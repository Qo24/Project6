Our story

For over 5 years, Sova Cafe has been catering some of the best cofee both esthetically and tastefully in Sarajevo. From its humble beginnings as a homegrown family business in the heart of the city of Sarajevo, our exceptional service and attention to detail and satisfaction of customers has managed to allow us to steadily grow to become one of Sarajevo's best cafees. Whether it is a corporate function, upscale event or a family gathering Sova Cafe provides great atmosphere along with its catering service that fit both your vision of how the event should go thorugh and it also fits your budget. Our well experienced staff will provide you and your event with the care and attention that it deserves.
